Reminders

Keep track of your tasks directly within your chat. Type /remind me to... [time] in chat to set a new reminder.

For example: "/remind me to call John tomorrow at 3pm". From here, you can also sync them to your Google, Outlook, or Apple Calendar!

Create a new skill automatically by typing /skill [what it does] in chat, or add one manually below by specifying a chat trigger command (e.g. /email).
